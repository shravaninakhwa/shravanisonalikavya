How our website relates with SDG 15

SDG 15 – Life on Land focuses on protecting, restoring, and promoting sustainable use of terrestrial ecosystems. ECO Connect supports this goal by offering a platform that encourages tree plantation drives, wildlife protection awareness, and biodiversity mapping. By connecting people with real environmental actions, ECO Connect becomes a bridge between communities and nature, helping conserve ecosystems and promote a greener, more sustainable planet.
